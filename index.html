Driving Change Through Innovation

Driven by personal loss, Duncun Motanya launched a transformative journey in healthcare. As a certified diabetes educator and remedial nurse, he founded the Diabetes & Hypertension Support Group Kenya in 2014, which has grown into a comprehensive healthcare ecosystem.

Today, Duncun combines his healthcare expertise with fintech leadership, advocating for policy frameworks and accelerating financial inclusion across Kenya's evolving ecosystem.
