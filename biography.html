Biography

The Journey of a Healthcare Innovator & Fintech Leader

Early Life & Education

Duncun Motanya's journey began with a deep-rooted passion for helping others and a keen interest in healthcare. His early experiences shaped his understanding of the critical need for accessible healthcare services, particularly in underserved communities.

His educational journey led him to pursue nursing, where he developed specialized expertise in diabetes care and chronic disease management. This foundation would later become the cornerstone of his innovative healthcare initiatives.

Nursing Education

Specialized training in diabetes care and chronic disease management

Certified Diabetes Educator

Professional certification in diabetes education and patient care

Career Timeline

2014

The Beginning: Personal Loss & Purpose

Driven by a personal loss, Duncun founded the Diabetes & Hypertension Support Group Kenya. What started as a grassroots effort to provide support and education has grown into one of Kenya's most impactful healthcare communities.

13,000+ Members Community Building
2018

Digital Transformation

Recognizing the power of technology in healthcare, Duncun launched the Kenya Diabetes Management Centre & Pharmacy digital platform. This innovative approach brought healthcare education and support to a broader audience through digital channels.

100K+ Followers Digital Innovation
2020

Physical Healthcare Facility

The vision became reality with the establishment of the Diabetes Management Medical Centre, Pharmacy & Laboratory (DMMC) in Ngara, Nairobi. This comprehensive facility combines traditional healthcare with modern telemedicine and e-pharmacy services.

Medical Centre Telemedicine E-Pharmacy
2024

Fintech Leadership

Duncun's expertise and leadership were recognized when he was appointed Chairperson of the Fintech Association of Kenya (FINTAK). In this role, he charts the strategic vision for Kenya's evolving fintech ecosystem and advocates for policy frameworks that promote financial inclusion.

FINTAK Chairperson Policy Advocacy Financial Inclusion
