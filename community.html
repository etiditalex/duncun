Making a Difference, One Life at a Time

Duncun Motanya's impact extends far beyond individual achievements. Through his leadership in healthcare and fintech, he has created lasting change that touches thousands of lives across Kenya and beyond. Healthcare Transformation

Diabetes & Hypertension Support Group

Founded in 2014, this grassroots initiative has grown to over 13,000 members, providing education, peer support, and resources for chronic disease management.

13,000+ Active Members
100+ Monthly Meetings

Digital Health Platform

The Kenya Diabetes Management Centre & Pharmacy digital platform reaches over 100,000 followers with educational content, health tips, and support resources.

100K+ Digital Followers
24/7 Online Support

Medical Centre & Telemedicine

The Diabetes Management Medical Centre in Ngara, Nairobi, provides comprehensive healthcare services including telemedicine consultations and e-pharmacy services.

5,000+ Patients Annually
500+ Telemedicine Consultations

Financial Inclusion & Fintech Leadership

Advancing Financial Inclusion

As Chairperson of the Fintech Association of Kenya, Duncun leads initiatives that promote financial inclusion across underserved regions and communities. His work focuses on creating accessible financial services that empower individuals and businesses.

Policy Advocacy
