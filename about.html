About Duncun Motanya

Healthcare Innovator, Fintech Leader, and Community Changemaker

Transforming Healthcare Through Innovation

Duncun Motanya stands at the intersection of healthcare innovation and financial technology, driving transformative change across Kenya's evolving landscape. As both a healthcare professional and fintech leader, he embodies the rare combination of clinical expertise and strategic business acumen.

His journey began with a personal loss that ignited a passion for healthcare accessibility. Today, he leads multiple organizations that collectively serve over 100,000 individuals, demonstrating his commitment to scaling impact through technology and innovation.

Current Leadership Roles

Chairperson

Fintech Association of Kenya (FINTAK)

Leading strategic vision for Kenya's fintech ecosystem, advocating for policy frameworks, and nurturing cross-sector collaboration to accelerate financial inclusion.

  • Strategic planning and policy advocacy
  • Cross-sector collaboration initiatives
  • Financial inclusion programs
  • Industry representation and lobbying

Founder & CEO

Diabetes Management Medical Centre (DMMC)

Operating a comprehensive healthcare facility in Ngara, Nairobi, combining physical clinic services with telemedicine, e-pharmacy, and patient support systems.

  • Clinical operations management
  • Telemedicine platform development
  • E-pharmacy services
  • Patient support group coordination

Founder

Diabetes & Hypertension Support Group Kenya

Managing a grassroots community that has grown to over 13,000 members, providing education, support, and resources for diabetes and hypertension management.

  • Community building and engagement
  • Educational program development
  • Peer support coordination
  • Resource distribution and management

Professional Background

Healthcare Expertise

As a certified diabetes educator and remedial nurse, Duncun brings deep clinical knowledge to his leadership roles. His healthcare journey spans over a decade, during which he has developed specialized expertise in chronic disease management, patient education, and healthcare system optimization.
